The Midwest Collegiate Taekwondo Conference is a gathering of the Midwestern States to further the experience and improvement of taekwondo. It is host to an annual taekwondo tournament open to all belts and ages. The Conference began in 2015, and the original website was developed by Master Nick Messersmith and Katrina Phonasa. The new website can be found here: www.mctc-tkd.com.
